## Open a Coverage Database

CovSight reads `.cdb` files — NCDB (Native Coverage DataBase) files produced by simulation tools.

**To open a database:**
1. Click the **Open Coverage Database** button in the Coverage Databases panel, or
2. Run the command `CovSight: Open Coverage Database` from the Command Palette (`Ctrl+Shift+P`).

CovSight will also **automatically discover** `.cdb` files in your workspace on startup.

> **Tip:** Configure `covsight.filePatterns` to customize which files are discovered.
