## Coverage Dashboard

The dashboard gives you a quick overview of coverage quality:

- **Overall Coverage Bar** — total bins covered vs. goal
- **Per-Type Breakdown** — coverage split by scope type (toggle, branch, coverpoint, etc.)
- **Uncovered Hotspots** — the bins with the lowest hit counts that are not yet covered

Open the dashboard from the Coverage Hierarchy panel toolbar or with `CovSight: Show Coverage Dashboard`.

> **Tip:** Set `covsight.coverageGoal` to your project target (e.g. `95`) to see which areas fall short.
